Our Estimators

Guider


Guider helps you with guidance that gives better understanding over a subject and effort that is required for each subject, to score and understand the core concepts and how subjects progress impacts both in industry knowledge and CGPA with resource links provided

Tracker


Tracker is a tool that takes input of your current semester marks for all your subjects and predicts your GPA based on the SEE marks you predict and enter for each subject, which makes it easier to students to analyze which subject to focus and how to study and score good

Attendance


AttendanceCal is a tool which will help you analyze how many classes you need to attend to attain a certain percentage, after taking leave for a long duration, it's annoying to use calculator apps calculating for attendace percentage after everyclass.

Predictor


Predictor is a ML model based on previous year data, which may vary but still can predict more close to the actual result in most cases, this process is done using neural networks which analyze the data and predict gpa based on hardness levels you chose for each subjects
