Welcome to Tracker.io


Tracker.io is a tool made by our CalGPA team which focuses on bringing awareness in students that even a single mark can change their grade by minus one. Using ths tool, students can now estimate their predicted GPA based on their inputs and then creating goals for the SEE and CIA Marks in the forms below




Instructions:

Step-1: Enter number of credited subjects you have
Step-2: Then you'll find new n boxes (for n subjects) fill the info
Step-3: You're almost done choose your options based on questions
Step-4: There you go your estimation is done!
