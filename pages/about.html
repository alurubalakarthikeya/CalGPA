About Us


CalGPA was created with a mission to give students an intuitive and reliable tool for assessing their academic performance. We understand that true academic success is more than just grades—it’s about the overall quality of the learning experience. Our platform empowers students by providing precise SGPA estimations based on their internal marks. CalGPA integrates machine learning and data analysis to offer highly accurate predictions. By leveraging historical data and sophisticated algorithms, our platform provides users with a personalized assessment of their potential SGPA. Whether you’re looking to gauge your current standing or set academic goals, CalGPA delivers insights that help you make informed decisions about your studies. With a user-friendly interface, students can easily input their internal marks, access detailed estimations, and track their academic journey like never before. CalGPA is here to simplify the way you approach your grades, giving you a clearer picture of your academic performance and helping you stay on top of your goals.

Q&A's


How does CalGPA work?

CalGPA uses historical data to analyze your performance, identify areas for improvement, and provide personalized SGPA estimations. Our machine learning algorithms analyze your marks, grades, and other relevant factors to predict your SGPA. We also incorporate a variety of factors like your academic performance, extracurricular activities, and coursework to provide a more accurate and comprehensive estimation.

Benefits of using CalGPA?

CalGPA offers several benefits to students. Firstly, it provides a reliable and accurate way to assess their performance. Secondly, it helps students identify areas for improvement and make necessary adjustments to their academic plans. Thirdly, it allows students to compare their SGPA with their peers and make informed decisions about their academic success.

Services by CalGPA?

CalGPA offers a variety of features and services to help you improve your academic performance. Some of the features and services include: 1. Input your internal marks and receive personalized SGPA estimations. 2. View your progress and analyze your academic performance. 3. Compare your SGPA with your peers and make informed decisions about your academic success. 4. Access a library of resources and information to help you improve your education.
