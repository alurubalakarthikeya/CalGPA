About Us


CalGPA was born out of a desire to provide students with a reliable and accurate way to assess their performance. We believe that academic success is not only determined by grades, but also by the quality of the learning experience. We have developed a sophisticated platform that allows users to input their internal marks and receive personalized SGPA estimations. CalGPA is a cutting-edge technology-driven project designed to help students estimate their grades based on their internal marks. Our team is working on machine learning and data analysis has developed an advanced algorithm that uses historical data to predict your SGPA. With our platform, you can easily access our estimations.

Our features


Estimator


Certainly works for 2nd and 3rd years, our GPA estimator estimates your current year CGPA based on your previous semester results, number of subjects you are good at and current performance (upto CIA's), but your performance and efforts you put will still play an important role!

Tracker


Tracker analyzes your current semester grades and provides valuable insights that can help you get better and also helps you work on important skills from subjects that are useful for IT sector and provides the order of importance to subjects in which you have to improve

Guider


Guider helps you with guidance that gives better understanding over each subject and effort that is required for each subject, to score and understand the core concepts and how high credit subjects progress impacts both in industry knowledge and CGPA

Predictor


This prediction is based on previous year data, which may vary but still can be more close to the actual result in most cases, this process is done using neural networks which analyze the data and predict yours based on hardness levels you chose for each subjects provides output
