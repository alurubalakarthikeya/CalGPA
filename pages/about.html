Q&A's


How CalGPA came out?

The idea for CalGPA originated from my personal experience. During my second semester, I was too relaxed about my sem-end exams and ended up ruining my GPA without realizing it. This experience made me understand the importance of keeping track of academic performance throughout the semester. To help other students avoid the same mistake, I decided to create CalGPA, a tool that provides an easy way to calculate and monitor GPA, ensuring that students can stay informed and make better decisions about their studies.

Challenges faced

I started working on this project to build my JavaScript skills which I think really did work. I believe that working on at least one project related to any of the subjects in a sem will definitely brings better understanding of that subject and makes you stand out in a group.

Why CalGPA helps?

CalGPA is designed to assist students in calculating and tracking their Grade Point Average (GPA) efficiently. By providing an easy-to-use interface and accurate calculations, CalGPA helps students stay on top of their academic performance, set realistic goals, and make informed decisions about their studies. Whether you are aiming for academic excellence or just trying to maintain a good standing, CalGPA is a valuable tool to help you achieve your educational objectives.
